About Me

My name is Jayden Shipley and I graduated Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Science in Digital Cinema Production at Utah Valley University in 2023.

I work on films as the Cinematographer, as well as 1st and 2nd Camera Assistant. In addition to Cinematography, I enjoy working in the Art Department and I've worked on projects such as The Book of Mormon Video Series, The Chosen, Stage Kids, and Jailbirds in various positions.

I also have a passion for photography, and I've enjoyed taking photos since I was in High School. I've recently taken up film photography as well. I am currently a certified Production Assistant with the Utah Film Commission.

A few films that inspire me and the work I want to create:

Fantastic Mr. Fox
Fantastic Mr. Fox is a quirky, stop-motion animated film directed by Wes Anderson, based on Roald Dahl's classic children's book. I love how the story is told in the stop motion medium. It creates a kind of wonder that really helps to convey the meaning of the childrens classic. From a child to a young adult, I have always enjoyed the set decoration, character development, and cinematography that make this film what it is.
Baby Driver
Baby Driver is a high-octane crime thriller from director Edgar Wright, where the title character, Baby, is a young getaway driver. Edgar Wright uses music to choreography the movement in this film. He is so deliberate with everything that he does down to the tiniest detail. I really enjoy his usage of music, and the fast pased, creative editing that is seen throuought this film.
The Secret Life of Walter Mitty
The Secret Life of Walter Mitty tells the story of Walter, a mild-mannered photo editor who escapes his dull life by daydreaming about grand adventures. This film is my all time favorite. Along with a teriffic storyline, this film also presents beautiful imagery and silly yet realistic portrayals of everyday people.
